About Us

Catalyst for economic transformation in Africa

NOVA PHENIX Group stands out through its systemic approach. We are not just a group of companies, but a growth partner operating between Algeria, Senegal and West Africa.

Our strength lies in the synergy of our 5 branches, enabling us to support our clients across the entire value chain: from space design to team training, through events, equipment imports and travel logistics.
